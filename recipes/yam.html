Boiled Yam with Shredded Fish and Veggie Sauce Recipe

Description

This is a simple and delicious recipe for Boiled Yam with Shredded Fish and Veggie Sauce. The yam is boiled until tender, then served with a flavorful sauce made from shredded fish and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yam (about 1 kg)
  • Water (enough to cover the yam)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 300g cooked fish (mackerel, tuna, croaker, or salmon), deboned and shredded
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 medium tomatoes, blended or finely chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers (tatashe), blended
  • 2 Scotch bonnet peppers (ata rodo), blended (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ½ teaspoon curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, green bell pepper, and green beans, diced)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped spring onions or parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the yam and cut it into thick slices or chunks.
  2. Place the yam in a pot and cover with water. Add salt if desired.
  3. Bring to a boil and cook for about 20-25 minutes, or until the yam is tender when pierced with a fork.
  4. While the yam is cooking, heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Sauté the sliced onions for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  6. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, then cook for about 1 minute.
  7. Stir in the blended tomatoes, bell peppers, and Scotch bonnet peppers.
  8. Cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and the oil begins to separate.
  9. Add the seasoning cube, dried thyme, curry powder, and salt. Mix well.
  10. Stir in the shredded fish and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  11. Add the diced mixed vegetables and cook for another 3-5 minutes. The vegetables should remain slightly crisp.
  12. Stir in the chopped spring onions or parsley just before removing the pan from the heat.
  13. Serve the boiled yam hot with the shredded fish and veggie sauce on top or on the side.
