Lemon and Sardine Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a simple and delicious recipe for Lemon and Sardine Pasta. The pasta is tossed with flaky sardines, fresh lemon juice and zest, olive oil, and a handful of parsley for a light, fresh finish. It's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner when you want something full of flavor without spending hours in the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or linguine
  • 1 can of sardines in olive oil
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about a minute.
  5. Open the can of sardines and add them to the pan, breaking them apart with a fork.
  6. Stir in the lemon juice and zest.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss everything together until well combined.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
